About

Most data engineers start with pipelines. I started with statistics, and it shows in what I build.

I'm the sole data professional at the District Monitoring Unit, Government of Tamil Nadu, reporting directly to the District Collector, which means I own everything: requirements, pipeline design, warehousing, dashboards, and the 6am fixes when a source sheet changes shape without warning. That constraint is where I learned to build for reliability first.

An MSc in Statistics from Madras Christian College shapes what I validate and what I compute, not just how I move data around. It's the difference between a pipeline that runs and one that surfaces what actually matters in the data.
